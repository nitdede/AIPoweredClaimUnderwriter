PREMIUM AND COST SHARING STRUCTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Deductible
Annual Deductible: INR 2,000

The deductible shall be applied once per policy year and shall be borne by the insured before any claim becomes payable under this policy.

2. Co-payment
A co-payment of 10% shall apply on the following:
- OPD consultation expenses
- Diagnostic and investigative expenses

No co-payment shall apply for in-patient hospitalization claims.

3. Out-of-Pocket Expenses
Out-of-pocket expenses include:
- Deductible amount
- Applicable co-payment
- Expenses beyond sub-limits
- Expenses not covered under the policy

------------------------------------------------------------
BENEFITS COVERED
------------------------------------------------------------

1. In-Patient Hospitalization
Expenses incurred for hospitalization exceeding 24 consecutive hours due to illness or injury are covered, including:
- Room rent up to INR 5,000 per day
- ICU charges up to INR 10,000 per day
- Medical practitioner fees
- Nursing charges
- Operation theatre charges
- Anesthesia
- Medicines and consumables
- Diagnostic tests related to hospitalization

2. Day Care Procedures
The following procedures are covered even if hospitalization is less than 24 hours:
- Cataract surgery
- Dialysis
- Chemotherapy
- Radiotherapy
- Angiography
- Minor surgical procedures as prescribed by a registered medical practitioner

3. Diagnostic Tests (OPD)
Diagnostic tests are covered when prescribed by a registered medical practitioner, including:
- Blood tests
- Pathology investigations
- X-Ray
- Ultrasound
- CT Scan
- MRI

Sub-limit for diagnostics:
Maximum INR 25,000 per policy year, subject to deductible and co-payment.

4. OPD Consultation
Consultation expenses incurred with a registered medical practitioner or specialist are covered.

Sub-limit for OPD consultation:
Maximum INR 15,000 per policy year, subject to deductible and co-payment.

------------------------------------------------------------
EXCLUSIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Permanent Exclusions
The following treatments and procedures are permanently excluded and shall not be payable under this policy under any circumstances:

- Cosmetic or aesthetic procedures including but not limited to:
  Plastic surgery
  Hair transplant
  Botox and fillers
  Liposuction, unless required due to accidental injury

- Dental treatment unless arising from an accident

- Fertility and assisted reproduction treatments including IVF and IUI

- Weight reduction or bariatric surgery unless medically necessary with BMI greater than 35 and associated co-morbidities

- Self-inflicted injuries or intentional harm

2. Waiting Period Exclusions
- Pre-existing diseases: Covered after a waiting period of 24 months
- Specified illnesses (hernia, cataract, gall bladder stones): Covered after 12 months

CLAIM CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

- All claims must be submitted within 30 days from the date of discharge or completion of treatment
- Original invoices, prescriptions, and diagnostic reports must be submitted
- Cashless treatment facility is available at network hospitals
- Non-network hospital claims shall be settled on reimbursement basis

------------------------------------------------------------
GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Medically Necessary
Treatment shall be considered medically necessary if it is required to diagnose or treat an illness or injury and is prescribed by a qualified medical practitioner.

2. Policy Renewal
This policy may be renewed annually subject to applicable terms and premium payment.

3. Fraud and Misrepresentation
Any claim arising from fraud, misrepresentation, or suppression of material facts shall be rejected and the policy may be cancelled.
